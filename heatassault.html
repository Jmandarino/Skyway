Heat Assault

Heat Assault

Our 100% effective and 100% GREEN solution for bed bugs and bed bug eggs.

What is Heat Assault?

By heating each room over 125°F (52°C), Heat Assault kills all bed bugs, and their eggs. The heat is uniformly distributed to ensure the elimination of all bed bugs and their eggs in a matter of seconds.

An Environmentally Friendly Approach

Heat Assault uses environmentally friendly heat transfer fluid (HTF), along with bio-diesel or diesel fuel to ensure minimal impact to the environment.

The HTF is heated by oil fired burners to 205°F (96°C) (well over the temperature needed to kill bed bugs), and stored in a reservoir. After storage, the HTF is pumped into industrial grade hoses into radiators which then heat the room.

Instead of harmful chemicals, we use heat treatment. Heating your residence or property to well over the kill range, including all hard to reach areas. Using only heat, residents can immediately return to their home. This peace of mind is simply not possible with chemical treatments.

Heat treatment is both pet and people friendly!
