Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

A Long Term Solution to Pest Problems

Integrated Pest Management (IPM) combines intelligent decision-making with a variety of preventive and corrective measures to reduce pests. IPM does not rely exclusively on the use of conventional insecticide sprays, but a more GREEN solution.

Who We Service

  • Commercial, retail and warehouse
  • Hotels and Motels
  • Bakeries, Restaurant, Food Processing Facility
  • Doctor's offices and hospitals
  • Apartments, condominiums and co-ops

A Team Effort

The team approach to IPM involves YOU and US working together

Insects such as cockroaches, ants and termites often cannot be eliminated quickly due to population size, biological characteristics and instinctive behavior for self-preservation. Often insect survivors escape the treatment zone and either remain concealed until pesticide residues break down or they invade new areas of the building.

Integrated Pest Management or IPM methods may or may not eliminate pests quickly, however, IPM can yield more enduring results in the long run than non-targeted, copious pesticide applications.

With your cooperation, and your knowledge of your problem, we will work as a team to solve your pest problem. This will help insure optimal results!

A High Tech Solution

Skyway Pest Management uses the latest technology to customize a service to suit all of your pest control needs. Gone are the days of spraying and fogging. Technology and science has enabled us to treat most problems more efficiently and effectively with no odor and virtually no impact to our environment.

Our highly-trained service technicians use advanced application techniques and products that control pests in your home or business.

How you can help us:

  • Keep your living and work space clean and uncluttered
  • Note and report exact locations of where insect pests are seen
  • Please refrain from using over-the-counter insecticides. Repellent insecticides alarm pests, which then escape and hide in structural voids
  • Examine used appliances, entertainment systems, furniture and storage boxes for evidence of pests before bringing such items indoors
  • Inspect the area and interview to determine if pest situation is improving
  • Remove food waste and empty beverage containers daily
  • Allow us access to pest entry points if need be
